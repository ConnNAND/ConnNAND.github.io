Education

College served as a great opportunity to work with others and to practice working collaberatively on projects at different scales for class, but also recreationally during events like game jams. Tackling these projects introduced me to concepts like scrum, the agile workflow, and to tools like Trello and ClickUp, as well as keeping me aquainted with weekly stand-ups.

Through these projects I have also gained plenty of management experience, taking part in meetings, but also organizing them, delegating work, and supporting others when they have questions or need assistance.
