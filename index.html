About Me

I graduated from Shawnee State University receiving a bachelor's degree in Digital Simulation & Game Programming. This formal education developed my skills and introduced me to many topics, tools, and languages including Python, C, C++, C#, HTML & CSS, Javascript, GLSL, Assembly, git, Perforce, Godot & GDScript, Unreal Engine 4 & 5, Unity, Blender, Maya, and the Adobe Suite, as well as audio engineering tools like Audacity, Reaper, Ableton, and experience recording with professional equipment.

I was also introduced to scrum and gained experience working in an agile environment, complimented with project management tools like Trello and ClickUp.
