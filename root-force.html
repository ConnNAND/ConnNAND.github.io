Root Force

Engine Used: Godot 3

Time Frame: February 3rd 2023 - February 5th 2023

Link: Itch.io

About the Project

This project was my team's entry into the 2023 Global Game Jam. In Root Force, you must survive wave after wave of living plants by defeating them with your chain-whacker. Aim high for tall enemies and aim low for short ones, all while trying to keep your distance and avoiding getting caught surrounded or cornered.

My main takeaway from Root Force is the value in team collaboration, with everything moving smoothly when everyone is comfortable communicating.

My primary contributions were the player controller, sound design, project management, and your cute, hungry, son.

Accomplishments & Lessons Learned

This project had me balancing teaching with creating. Most of my group had not touched a game engine until this point so I showed them the ropes while assigning work based on what they should have been able to handle, all the while making contributions of my own.

I managed to program the player controller, help out with the map, and create the AI for your son, on top of the sound design.

Notably for the sound design, the "chain-whacker" that the player wields was the first time I needed to create looping audio outside of music, so figuring out how to have it wind-up, sustain, and release was a bit of a challenge, but it turned out alright. It also changes pitch while carving into enemies, a delightful little touch that added to the immersion.
